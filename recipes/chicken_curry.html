Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken cooked in an Indian style and mixed with spices to make a thick soup, hence the name curry.

Chicken curry in most cases is served with rice, but can also go well with mashed potatoes or pasta.

Ingredients

  • Chicken preferable boneless.
  • 3 large capsicums(red, yellow and green).
  • Carrots.
  • White onions, garlic and ginger.
  • Curry powder, salt, bay leaves.
  • Heavy cream.
  • (Optional) Black Pepper.

Steps

  1. After washing your chicken, bring it boil for about 10 minutes.
  2. Chop off the white onions into very small pieces and in a pan of hot oil add them.
  3. Cut the capsicums and blend them. Crash ginger and garlic into a thick paste. Finely chop the carrots into small cubes.
  4. Take the chicken off the boiling pan and let it cool for about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the garlic and ginger paste into the onions and let that fry for a minute. Be careful not to burn that as garlic burns easily.
  6. Add the carrots, curry powder, bay leaves, salt, pepper and the chicken together and let them cook for about 30 minutes.
  7. Add a little bit of cream to make the soup thick. You can use yorghut if no heavy cream
  8. Tip: This meal works well with mashed potatoes. Which you will can find on my Mashed Potatoes Recipe.
