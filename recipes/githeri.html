Githeri

Description

Githeri is a Kenyan meal where you boil beans and maize together thoroughly.

After this pre-cook it is then fried to your own liking.

Ingredients

  • Beans and maize
  • 2 Capsicums
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • (Optional) Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Chop of all the veggies.
  2. In a pan of hot oil
  3. After around 20 minutes the potatoes should be ready, take out and sieve out the rest of the water if any remained.
  4. Take a masher murphy and mash the potatoes. Add salt to taste and add cream and milk in bits as you contionue mashing.
  5. When the mixture is to your liking add a touch of the basil or corrianders and serve while hot.
  6. Tip: This meal works well with chicken curry. Which you will can find on my Chicken Curry Recipe.
